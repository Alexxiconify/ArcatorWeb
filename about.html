About Arcator.co.uk

Our Story and Mission

Arcator.co.uk has been a thriving Minecraft community since its inception in 2012. We pride ourselves on creating a friendly, engaging, and stable environment for Minecraft players of all ages and playstyles.

Our mission is to foster a strong community where players can connect, build, explore, and embark on epic adventures together. We believe in fair play, respect, and continuous improvement to deliver the best possible Minecraft experience.

For newcomers, we offer a dedicated Freebuild Creative server for unrestricted building, allowing you to unleash your imagination without limits. We also have a robust survival server with added plugins to enhance your gameplay.

You can also check out our Wiki for more historical information, including our official trailer from 2016 and older videos dating back to 2010 and 2012, showcasing our journey through the years.

Areas for Growth

Immediate Growth & Development:

  • Seek unique ideas to grow and discuss new concepts for modded Minecraft.
  • Prioritize community games equally with Minecraft.
  • Create unique channels/forums/threads for small social groups and ice breakers (e.g., Watch2gether).
  • Host Game Nights to build and strengthen friendships.
  • Increase frequency and consistency of games through better event coordination.
  • For SSMP: Find community activities (goals/minigames/events) to keep it fresh.
  • Weekly anime movie nights for a successful clique example.
  • Encourage forums for sharing interests.
  • Rocket League has great potential.
  • Explore other games like Valorant, Siege, Brawlhalla, Overwatch, Phasmophobia, Space Engineers, and Terraria.

Continued & Backburner Growth:

  • Keep the community census up-to-date to help members find common interests.
  • Advertising: Currently deprioritized server-wide, but individual ads are welcomed.
  • Server merges: Deprioritized but always open for new ideas.
  • Consider reviving the Arcator YouTube channel, allowing members to practice cinematography.
  • For other Minecraft aspects: Explore mod packs (Beta in 1.19, Beta Mod), adventure maps.
  • Nostalgia: Provide maps and encourage sharing screenshots of historical/cool builds.
