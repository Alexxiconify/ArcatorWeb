About Arcator.co.uk

Our Journey, Mission, and Community Values

Our Story & Mission

Arcator.co.uk began its journey in **October 2012**, born from a passion for Minecraft and a desire to create a stable, engaging, and friendly online community. What started as a small venture quickly grew into a vibrant hub for players seeking unique experiences beyond the standard game.

Our mission is simple: to provide a long-lasting, fun, and safe environment for Minecraft players of all ages and playstyles. We believe in fostering a community where creativity flourishes, friendships are made, and every player feels welcome and valued. We are committed to continuous improvement, adapting to the evolving landscape of Minecraft while retaining the core values that have defined us for over a decade.

Over the years, we've hosted countless adventures, witnessed incredible builds, and built a dedicated global community. We are passionate about Minecraft and even more passionate about the community we've built together.

Our Core Values

Community First

We prioritize a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere where every member feels respected and heard. Our community's feedback drives our development.

Innovation & Fun

We constantly explore new ways to enhance the Minecraft experience, introducing fresh content, challenges, and events to keep things exciting for our players.

Stability & Reliability

Our robust infrastructure and dedicated administration ensure a smooth, low-lag gameplay experience with high uptime and secure data.

Transparency & Communication

We believe in open communication with our community, providing regular updates on server status, development, and decisions that affect our players.

What's Happening in Our Community:

  • **Regular Events:** From building competitions to mini-game tournaments, there's always something fun happening.
  • **Voice Chat & Hangouts:** Connect with other members in our Discord voice channels during game sessions or just to chat.
  • **Community Suggestions:** We actively listen to player feedback and implement suggestions to improve the server.
  • **Collaborative Builds:** Participate in large-scale community projects and leave your mark on the Arcator world.
  • **Creative Cinematography:** Utilize features from the Arcator YouTube channel, allowing members to practice cinematography.
  • **Other Minecraft aspects:** Explore mod packs (Beta in 1.19, Beta Mod), adventure maps.
  • **Nostalgia:** Provide maps and encourage sharing screenshots of historical/cool builds.
