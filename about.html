Our Story & Mission

Arcator.co.uk started in October 2010 from a passion for Minecraft. We've grown from a small venture into a vibrant community hub.

Our mission is to provide a fun, safe environment for Minecraft players. We foster creativity and friendships while upholding our core values.

Our Core Values

Community First

Welcoming atmosphere where every player feels respected.

Innovation & Fun

Explore new ways to enhance Minecraft with fresh content.

Stability & Reliability

Robust infrastructure for smooth, low-lag gameplay with high uptime.

Communication

Open communication about server development and community decisions.
