Setup Instructions

Follow these steps to configure EmailJS for your website:

  1. Sign up for a free account at EmailJS.com
  2. Create an email service (Gmail, Outlook, or custom SMTP)
  3. Create an email template
  4. Get your Public Key from the EmailJS dashboard
  5. Enter your Public Key below and test the connection
