SSMP (Strawberry Survival Multiplayer)

SSMP, or Strawberry SMP, is a vanilla survival multiplayer server where you can connect with others, build together, form teams, or embark on solo adventures. Wars are not a part of this peaceful experience. We invite you to join this history-rich server!

History:

The SSMP began in summer 2019, sparking a revival for Arcator after a period of inactivity. Around 20 friends, many reuniting after years, came together to create this new SMP, eventually naming it Strawberry SMP.

The server's first day saw players establishing bases around a small village on a cape, with some venturing to other islands. Road systems, homes, and gardens flourished, and a thriving economy emerged, connecting everything through the Nether.

As the community grew, so did challenges. Pillager raids became a daily occurrence, uniting players to build walls and defend the village. These raids transformed into community events, strengthening bonds as players fought side-by-side.

Despite a period of decline and a server crash, the SSMP returned in December 2019 as a new server, bringing new players and activities. It continues to run actively today.

The Six Hour War:

A period of intense conflict, the "Six Hour War" saw cities burn and players band together. Despite initial desires for peace, war became inevitable, leading to a fierce struggle where communities fought to defend their territories.

Hub (MC1)

The original Arcator server, launched in December 2010, serving as the central hub for all adventures. It features diverse game modes and historical worlds.

  • Freebuild: Build without restrictions or claiming.
  • Plots: Build in specified land pieces with grief protection.
  • Peaceful Survival: Survival without monsters, griefing, or theft, with disabled fall damage. Explore builds and memories from past members.
  • Modified Survival: Basic survival with hostile mobs, but no PvP, raiding, or griefing. Features various plugins for unique gameplay.
  • Historical Worlds (since 2011), including "Sky City" (built 2013-2014)
  • Integrated Minigame Section
