Our Favorite Non-Minecraft Games

While Minecraft is our main hub, many Arcator community members enjoy playing a variety of other games together. Join our Discord to find groups, organize sessions, and discover new favorites!

Factorio

Build and maintain factories, automate production, and fend off alien creatures in this highly addictive simulation game. Perfect for those who love intricate logistics.

Satisfactory

An open-world factory building game with a first-person perspective. Explore an alien planet, harvest resources, and construct massive automated factories.

Kerbal Space Program

Design, build, and launch rockets and spacecraft to explore the Kerbal solar system. Master orbital mechanics and conquer the cosmos!

A grand strategy game set in space. Explore a vast galaxy, encounter diverse alien races, and forge your empire through diplomacy, trade, or war.

Cities: Skylines

Build your dream city from the ground up! Manage services, traffic, and citizen happiness in this modern take on classic city simulation games.
