Beyond Minecraft: Other Games We Play

Discover More Fun!

While Minecraft is our core, our community enjoys a variety of other games and activities together!

Rocket League

Experience high-flying, car-based soccer! Rocket League combines vehicle chaos with the competitive thrill of football.

Join us for casual matches and competitive ranks.

Valorant

A tactical first-person shooter with unique agent abilities. Team up and outsmart your opponents in intense 5v5 matches.

Form a squad and climb the ranks together!

Terraria

Dig, fight, explore, and build in this sandbox adventure game. Terraria offers endless possibilities for creativity and discovery.

Collaborate on epic builds or embark on boss hunts!

Overwatch 2

Dive into a futuristic world where heroes battle in fast-paced, team-based combat. A dynamic hero shooter with diverse characters and roles.

Join our community for quickplay or competitive matches.

Phasmophobia

Gather your ghost-hunting gear and investigate haunted locations. A cooperative psychological horror game where you identify paranormal entities.

Perfect for spooky game nights with friends!

Space Engineers

Build and maintain space stations, planetary outposts, and spacecraft in a vast, open-world sandbox. Explore, mine, and survive in space.

Unleash your engineering creativity in multiplayer!
